Men Ministry

Mighty Men Of Valour (MMV)

As men are ordained as head of the home, there is need that we are sharpens and be responsible to face any challenge. As a result of this Men at Elim Temple instituted a scholarship scheme to support needy children in the church further their education

Precious Vessels Of Virtue (PVV)

PVV are sisters in one Body, whose mission is to see every woman develop into the fullness of the virtuous woman God intended for her to be. Our goals are to manifest the standards of Christ in every way; to grow in the Love of God; to promote oneness and unity with a sense of family; to represent the image and display the character of Jesus Christ.

We intend to achieve these goals by pursuing the following objectives: To grow in the grace and knowledge of God through Bible studies. To host edifying, fun-filled activities, such as luncheons, teas, coffees and retreats To enhance our personal relationship with God through monthly fellowships, events and seminars To encourage women to develop in the character of Christ To teach women to walk in wholeness through the Word of God — spirit, soul and body.

Youth Ministry

Its a ministry for all teenagers in the church. This ministry offers the platform and provide opportunities for them to know more of Jesus Christ in this early stage of life though regular Sunday session and other activities that exposes them to the thing of the Lord

Children Ministry

Children are our future. Recent events indicate that the world is becoming evil and darker around us, it’s extremely important and our responsibility to provide our children with a solid foundation in the word of God.At Elim Temple, a necessary foundation is built throughout each age level with the same emphasis as adults. Children are exposed to and taught the word of God at a tender age.

It is our belief and responsibility to position the saints in the world as leaders thoroughly equip with the word of God. This ministry provides nurturing and loving environment for infants, toddlers and teenagers to learn to hear the voice of God, know God and His purpose. Dominion Kids participate in Praise & Worship, Prayer, Bible studies, Story time, Music and many fun activities.

They learn to memorize and recite scriptures, reference the Bible for Salvation, Healing, Faith and Prayer etc.; and are able to apply the principles of leading someone to Christ. Much more Elim Kids know how to be occupied with Jesus. Our teams of Spirit-filled teachers are well equipped to provide the molding of children from toddler years to age thirteen.
