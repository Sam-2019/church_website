Welcome Message from
Rev. Robert deGraft-Johnson

It’s with great excitement and joy in welcoming you into ICGC, Elim Temple. Our believe and conviction is that as you fellowship with us, you will access your place of refreshing and comfort just as the Israelites received rest at Elim (Numbers 33:9) from the desert so shall it be for you in Jesus Name

About Us

Elim Temple is a young dynamic church and an extension of the International Central Gospel Church at Dahwenya near Community 25, Tema. Elim Temple celebrated its 5th Anniversary in 2018 since it moved to its current location at Terazzo Junction near Dawhenya.

A place of refreshing; ICGC-Elim Temple was started by Pastor Robert deGraft-Johnson as ICGC Dawhenya branch on 24th June 2012 with about 25 adults and few children. While fasting and praying for open heavens in the same month of that year, God revealed the name "Elim" in Numbers 33:9 to him "And they removed from Marah, and came to Elim: and in Elim were twelve fountains of water and threescore and ten palm tress and they pitched there" (KJV).

Today, to the Glory of God the church has a membership of over 200 believers. ICGC-Elim Temple is best described as a young church on the move.

Come and join us;
let us shift the Kingdom works onto a higher level.
